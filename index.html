About Me

I love to develop software, especially the programming part of the process. I’m currently focused on developing for the web because the W3C aims to make the Web accessible to everyone and every device.

I want to develop software that makes the lives of others better by helping people complete tasks easier and quicker so they can do more enjoyable activities. For example, Inferhythm helps get the lyrics from the website Genius for the track the user is currently listening to on Spotify.

Most of my experience is with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but I have worked with MongoDB, Express, and Node.js. I’m willing to learn anything to solve problems or improve the product.

I recently graduated from Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) with a Bachelor of Science.

When I'm not making websites, I enjoy playing basketball and video games, listening to music, or watching TV. I’m also developing a small mobile game with a friend for fun.
