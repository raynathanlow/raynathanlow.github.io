About Me

Hi, I'm Ray.

I'm a UX designer who strongly believes in using the iterative process of researching, prototyping, and testing in my work. I'm a recent graduate of the School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) program at Simon Fraser University.

In my free time, I like to play video games, watch TV, and listen to music. I prefer playing games I can zone out in like Path of Exile or Diablo III. Similarly, I enjoy watching sitcoms as they don't normally require a lot of thinking. Music-wise, I regularly listen to a lot of hip-hop, R&B, and pop.
