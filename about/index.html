About Me

Hi, I'm Ray.

I'm currently Simon Fraser University studying in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT). I’ve learned and practiced the iterative design process necessary to design user-friendly websites. This process involves a lot of research, prototyping, and testing which I'm grateful to have been exposed to since it can be used in designing other products.

Video games, for example, also benefit from the iterative design process. More recently, I’ve been trying to create some games in my free time because I find it fulfilling to complete a game and see others enjoy playing it.

Aside from trying to create games in my free time, I also like to play video games, watch TV, and listen to music. Since I’m mostly busy studying, I prefer playing games I can zone out in like Path of Exile or Diablo III. Similarly, I enjoy watching sitcoms as they don’t normally require a lot of thinking. Music-wise, I regularly listen to a lot of hip-hop, R&B, and pop.
